# Personal Photo Instructions

## 📸 Save Your Photo Here

**Save your personal photo as `profile.jpg` in this directory (`public/images/profile.jpg`)**

## 📋 Requirements

- **Filename**: `profile.jpg` (exactly, lowercase)
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Dimensions**: 800x1000px or similar 4:5 aspect ratio
- **File size**: Under 2MB for optimal performance
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional appearance

## 🎯 Usage

Your photo will be displayed in:

- **Hero Section**: Main homepage image with grayscale filter
- **About Section**: Profile image with floating animation

## 🎨 Effects Applied

- Grayscale filter (removes on hover in Hero section)
- Rounded corners and shadows
- Responsive scaling for all devices
- Dark/light mode compatible
- Smooth hover transitions

## ✅ After Saving

1. Save your photo as `public/images/profile.jpg`
2. Refresh your browser
3. Check both Hero and About sections
4. Verify the image displays correctly

Your personal photo will automatically replace all placeholder images!
